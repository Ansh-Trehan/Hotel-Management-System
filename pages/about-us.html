About Hotel Elysium

We gladly welcome you to the Hotel Elysium, a Budget Hotel in Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh. Enjoy the luxuries of this Hotel. Our hotel has each room equipped with a telephone, T.V. & cable television. Ask our helpful receptionists for assistance around the clock. Each and every guest enjoys the high speed wifi internet access included in all the rooms. Wireless internet connection is also available in all the public areas. Car rental is available at our hotel. Just ask the helpful team at the reception at your leisure. Our helpful staff will cater for your every need, e.g. a gentle wake-up call or a hearty meal through our room service. We are proud to offer our corporate travellers a modern business floor. Keep your jewellery, money or important documents secure in either the safety deposit box provided at reception or the one in your own fetchingly furnished room. Please indicate when making your booking if you require a non-smoking room, or a crib for your child. Relax on your own in the hotel's lively pub, or meet up with family or friends to discuss your day. Guests of the hotel enjoy relaxing in our lively hotel with family and friends. Stop in at our atmospheric restaurant for some sweet and savory treats!
